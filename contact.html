FAQs

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting in touch. Still unsure? Send us a message!

How quickly will you reply?

We aim to reply to all messages within 2–4 hours on working days (Mon–Sat, 8am–6pm IST). Messages received on Sunday are answered by 10am Monday.

Can I book a tour directly here?

Yes! Use the booking form on our Tours page to reserve your spot. You can also call us or send a message with your preferred tour and dates.

Do you offer corporate / team events?

Absolutely! We design custom team-building foraging experiences for groups of 10–50 people. These include guided expeditions, cooking workshops, and safety training sessions.

Are tours available in other languages?

Our guides speak English, Tamil, Malayalam, and Kannada. Tours in Tamil and Malayalam are available for select dates — please specify your preference when booking.
