What We Find

Commonly Foraged Species

A glimpse into the diverse fungi and wild edibles we encounter on our tours.

Edible

Chanterelle

Golden fruiting bodies with fruity aroma. Found under oak and pine in cooler months.

Medicinal

Reishi

Polypore known for immune-boosting properties. Found on decaying hardwood stumps.

Edible

Oyster Mushroom

Fan-shaped clusters on dead wood. Mild flavour, excellent for beginners to identify.

Caution

Death Cap

Amanita phalloides — world's most toxic mushroom. Never consume. Learn to identify it.
