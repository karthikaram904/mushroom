🌿 Our Story

About ForageWild

Connecting people with nature through safe, educational, and transformative foraging experiences since 2014.

Our Mission

Bridging Humans and the Natural World

ForageWild began as a small community of botanists and mycologists who believed that reconnecting with wild food is fundamental to human wellbeing. Today we lead 400+ tours annually across South India's most biodiverse regions.

🌱

Ecological Respect

We practice sustainable harvesting — taking only what we need and leaving habitats undisturbed for future generations.

🔬

Scientific Rigour

Every species taught has been verified through morphological and molecular identification by certified mycologists.

🤝

Community First

We partner with local tribal communities who hold centuries of traditional foraging knowledge and wisdom.

Our Journey

A Decade of Wild Discovery

1

2014 — Founded

Started as weekend walks in Ooty with 5 friends and a dog-eared field guide borrowed from the local library.

2

2017 — First Commercial Tours

Launched paid guided tours in the Nilgiris and Western Ghats with 3 certified mycologist guides.

3

2020 — Digital Field Guide

Launched our online species database with 500+ entries, now used by over 40,000 foragers across India.

4

2022 — Safety Certification

Became the first foraging company in South India to receive official Wilderness First Aid certification.

5

2024 — 2,400+ Members

A thriving foraging community spanning Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Goa, and beyond.
